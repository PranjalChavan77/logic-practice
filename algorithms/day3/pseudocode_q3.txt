A shop gives a discount to a customer if:
The day is not Sunday, AND
The purchase amount > 5000.
Write a pseudocode, and expected result to determine if the user gets a discount.

begin
    string day
    numeric purchase_amount

    display "Enter day:"
    accept day
    display "Enter purchase amount:"
    accept purchase_amount

    if (day != "Sunday") AND (purchase_amount > 5000) then
        display "Discount Applied"
    else
        display "No Discount"
    end if
end

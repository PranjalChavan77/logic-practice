Pseudocode to find the smallest of three numbers

begin
   numeric n1, n2, n3, least
   display "Enter three numbers:"

   accept n1, n2, n3

   least = n1

   if n2 < least then
      least = n2
   end if

   if n3 < least then
      least = n3
   end If

   display "The least number is ", least
end

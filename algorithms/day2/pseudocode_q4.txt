Write pseudocode to calculate and display the sum of the first 10 natural numbers.

begin
  numeric nNum, nSum

  nSum = 0
  nNum = 1

  display "Enter initial number:"
  accept nNum

  display "Enter initial sum:"
  accept nSum

  while nNum <= 10 do
      nSum = nSum + nNum
      nNum = nNum + 1
  end while

  display "The sum of first 10 natural numbers is:", nSum
end
